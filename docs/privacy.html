Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2025

Summary: OwlSeer Lite is designed with privacy in mind. Your chat history is stored locally on your device, and we do not collect or transmit your personal conversations to our servers.

1. Introduction

OwlSeer Lite ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information Stored Locally

  • Chat History: Your conversations with the AI assistant are stored locally on your device using iOS's secure storage mechanisms.
  • App Settings: Your preferences and configuration settings are stored locally.
  • API Keys: If you choose to use your own API keys, they are securely stored in iOS Keychain on your device.

2.2 Information Transmitted to Third Parties

  • AI Service Providers: When you send a message, your input is transmitted to AI service providers (such as Moonshot AI, OpenAI, Anthropic, or Google) to generate responses. These transmissions are encrypted using HTTPS.
  • Search Providers: If you enable web search features, your queries may be sent to search providers (Tavily or SerpAPI).

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our AI assistant service
  • To improve and optimize the user experience
  • To respond to your support requests

4. Data Storage and Security

We prioritize the security of your data:

  • All local data is stored using iOS's secure storage mechanisms
  • API keys are stored in iOS Keychain with hardware-level encryption
  • All network communications use HTTPS encryption
  • We do not maintain servers that store your personal data

5. Third-Party Services

Our app integrates with the following third-party AI services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Moonshot AI (Kimi) - Privacy Policy
  • OpenAI - Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic - Privacy Policy
  • Google (Gemini) - Privacy Policy
  • DeepSeek - Privacy Policy

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all data stored locally on your device
  • Delete: Delete your chat history and settings at any time through the app
  • Portability: Your data is stored locally and can be backed up through iOS backup mechanisms

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
